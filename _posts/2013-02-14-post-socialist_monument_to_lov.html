Flower sculpture set up for the Chinese Lunar New Year but perfect for Valentine's Day at the Haizhu District Wetland Park. The ecological park was officially opened this past October, but a large section of the park is still being completed. This park is part of a plan for a new Haizhu District Ecological Town.

Empty factories still stand across the river.

Local villagers fish with nets, and tourists crowd around with cameras.
